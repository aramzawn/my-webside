About Me:

As a Mechanical Engineering graduate in the final phase of my Master's program at the Ruhr University Bochum, I have honed my skills in Mechatronics, Electrical Engineering, IT, and Automation.

I am a passionate engineer, constantly striving to transcend the traditional boundaries of my field. My academic journey began at the prestigious Ruhr University Bochum, where I completed my Bachelor's in Mechanical Engineering with a specialization in Design and Automation Technologies.

My curiosity and desire to gain new experiences led me to a semester abroad in Turkey, where I acquired not just professional knowledge, but also interpersonal and social skills. This experience broadened my perspective and inspired me to seek further challenges.

Consequently, I started my professional internship at Cademy GmbH. During my internship, I gained deep insights into the world of Automation Technology, working with Controllino control units, autonomous transport systems, collaborative robots, and additive manufacturing. My performance there led to the opportunity to write my Bachelor's thesis on the development of a mobile collaborative robot system for the automotive industry. I had the privilege of leading the project independently and ultimately developed a prototype. This project was not only a milestone in my academic career, but also a platform to acquire extensive knowledge in control units, programming, sensors, electronics, mechatronics, and a understanding of industrial applications.

In my Master's program, I continued my path of excellence and achieved an outstanding grade point average of 3.8. Parallel to my studies, I worked as a research assistant. As a student employee at the Chair of Production Systems, I was able to not only deepen my presentation skills and technical knowledge, but also demonstrate my ability to work in international teams and advance projects independently. These experiences taught me the importance of each individual's contribution to the excellence of the final product.

Following my role at the chair, I completed another semester abroad in Malaysia. This experience reinforced my commitment to continuous development and intercultural exchange.

My interest in the forefront of technology led me to choose my Master's thesis: Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision for the automated recognition of assembly steps in the context of assistance systems, marking the culmination of my academic journey.

While working on my master's thesis, I seize the opportunity to work as a working student at Comma Soft AG in the field of IT Consulting, where I am expanding my knowledge in Microsoft DSC, Microsoft 365, and Azure. This position allows me to gain practical experience in IT consulting and deepen my understanding of digital workplace solutions, further broadening my horizon in the field of IT and automation technologies.

With a strong academic background, practical experience, and a passion for innovations in Artificial Intelligence and IT, I am ready to steer Mechanical Engineering in new, forward-looking directions. My journey is characterized by constant experience gathering, adaptability, and the desire to break traditional boundaries. I have sharpened my skills and knowledge over the years and am eager to continue expanding and learning .

With my commitment to continuous improvement and a performance-oriented mindset, I look forward to the next steps in my life!
