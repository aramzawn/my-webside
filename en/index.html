About Me

My academic journey began with a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering at the Ruhr University Bochum, where I specialized in design and automation technology. A semester abroad in Turkey not only expanded my technical knowledge but also enhanced my intercultural competencies and social skills.

For my bachelor's thesis, I developed a mobile collaborative robotic system for the automotive industry. In this independently led project, I was able to gain extensive experience in control technology, programming, sensor technology, electronics, and mechatronics. This project underscored my ability to tackle complex technical challenges and realize innovative solutions.

Continuing my path of excellence in my master's studies, I achieved an outstanding grade point average. Parallel to my studies, I took on a position as a research assistant. As a student employee at the Chair of Production Systems, I not only deepened my presentation skills and technical knowledge but also demonstrated my ability to work in international teams and drive projects independently.

Following my position at the chair, I completed another semester abroad in Malaysia. This experience reinforced my commitment to continuous development and intercultural exchange.

My interest in cutting-edge technologies led me to choose my master's thesis: "Artificial Intelligence and Computer Vision for the Automated Recognition of Assembly Steps in the Context of Assistance Systems", marking the pinnacle of my academic journey. In this work, I not only acquired deep knowledge in AI and computer vision but also developed an innovative system that contributes to the improvement of production processes. Through the application of deep learning and image processing techniques, I was able to realize solutions that can find practical application in the industry.

While working on my master's thesis, I seized the opportunity to work at Comma Soft AG as a working student in IT consulting, where I expanded my knowledge in Microsoft DSC, Microsoft 365, and Azure. By developing an automation script with Microsoft DSC, I significantly contributed to increasing the efficiency of internal processes. This position allowed me to gain practical experience in IT consulting and deepen my understanding of digital workplace solutions.

Currently, I am working as an IT Application Consultant at LIS Logistische Informationssysteme GmbH, where I serve as an interface between IT development and customers. With my expertise in scripting (Python), database management (SQL), and Microsoft Office, I optimize workflows and solve complex technical challenges. I lead projects that result in significant process improvements and automations, enhancing the efficiency of our customers. Through my ability to communicate technical solutions understandably, I support customers in implementing and optimally using IT systems.

With my passion for automation, artificial intelligence, and IT, as well as my experience in consulting and project management, I am ready to set innovative accents in the IT industry. I possess strong communication skills and a performance-oriented mentality that enable me to successfully master both technical and interpersonal challenges.

I look forward to further expanding my knowledge, exploring new technologies, and shaping the future of IT and automation together with you!
